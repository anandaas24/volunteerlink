The Volunteer Job Website is a technology-based solution created to facilitate management and participation in volunteer activities. This application provides a platform that connects individuals with volunteers who wish to contribute voluntarily. This website offers a variety of features, including Service, Experience, Adding a Portfolio, and you can also register in the form listed. , and can also contact the contacts listed. The Volunteer Jobs website aims to build a strong and organized volunteer community, as well as increase participation in volunteer activities. With this application, it is hoped that positive contributions to society and individuals in need will become more efficient and meaningful.
